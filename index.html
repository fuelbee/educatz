EDUCAZI
Continuous Professional Development for Teachers

Educazi, A fuelbee-exclusive teachers training program, is keen on enriching teachers with continual education programmes. We strongly believe that the empowerment of a teacher can benefit many students in need of academic guidance. We aim to reach as many teachers as possible, specifically in sub-urban & rural areas, to upgrade their knowledge & skills for enhanced competency in teaching, which consequently results in improved standards in Educational Institutions.

D.E.Ed

Diploma in English Education

D.E.Ed is for aspiring or professional English teachers who wish to acquire or upgrade their teaching skills to suit the contemporary standards demands of English education.They are Educated to adapt a holistic approach towards english language teaching, encompassing language enhancement & Classroom management skills.

Course Coverage

D.E.Ed packs comprehensive training modules that take teachers through 3 progressive levels

Course Outcome

By the end of this course, the aspirant would be a certified English teacher, proficient in

  1. Language structure & usage
  2. Learner assessment & teaching methodologies
  3. Lesson planning & Classroom management
